Commands Use In This Bot

  • g!play "title or URL of the song" = Play The Song You Need
  • g!pause = Pause The Music You Are Currently Playing
  • g!resume = Resume The Song That Has Paused
  • g!stop = Stop The Playing Song And Leave The Voice Channel
  • g!queue = Show The Songs Queue
  • g!skip = Skip The Current Song And Play The Next Song In The Queue
  • g!repeat on / g!repeat off = Repeat The Current Song Or Off The Repeat Song
  • g!volume = Increas Or Decrease The Volume Or Show The Current Volume.
  • g!help = Shows The Entire Commands In Bots
  • g!invite = Invite The Bot To Another Server
