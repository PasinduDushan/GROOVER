Commands Use In This Bot

  • !play "title or URL of the song" = Play The Song You Need
  • !pause = Pause The Music You Are Currently Playing
  • !resume = Resume The Song That Has Paused
  • !stop = Stop The Playing Song And Leave The Voice Channel
  • !search "Song Name Or URL" = Search The Entire Web And Give The Mathing Result For Input Song
  • !queue = Show The Songs Queue
  • !skip = Skip The Current Song And Play The Next Song In The Queue
  • !nowplaying Or !np = Show The Current Song Playing
  • !volume = Increas Or Decrease The Volume Or Show The Current Volume.
  • !help = Shows The Entire Commands In Bots
  • !invite = Invite The Bot To Another Server
